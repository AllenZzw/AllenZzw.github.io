Hello, I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer and Engineering of the Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with professor Jimmy Ho Man Lee. My research focus on constraint optimization and satisfaction. Before becoming a Ph.D. student, I obtained a bachelor of computer science degree (the First Class Honour) also from CUHK.

Research

Currently, my research focus on dominance relations in discrete constraint optimization problems. Identification and utilization of dominance relations usually require human expertise. My work is to automate the process to make dominance breaking accessible to the non-experts.
